      Aberystwyth & Ceredigion's Favourite Florist

      Established in 2013, No.21 Flowers is an independent Florist in Aberystwyth, West Wales with a difference. Alex often takes inspiration from the wild, creating bouquets and arrangements not just with colour but also texture. Embracing the seasons and using the best of what's available. We have a wide selection of bouquets, arrangements, plants and gifts- so we are confident we will have something you are looking for.

      New for 2021, our cut flower garden will be blossoming and we hope to fill the shop with welsh grown flowers during the summer months.

      Welcome to our beautiful shop

      No.21 Flowers has an enviable selection of indoor plants, from small cacti and succulents to larger statement plants. Pop in for a browse and see what we have. We often have some ready made terrariums and cacti arrangements in store. Our houseplant selection is constantly changing. If you are after a particular plant do ask as we can often order in although it may take a couple of weeks.

      Please note that during the winter months our shop is too cold for most house plants so we have very few available. Fresh stock will arrive in the Spring.

      Our location is conveniently within a short walk of the train and bus station and close to the main taxi rank. Just follow the one way system through the town and you'll find us on the right hand side. We are a bricks and mortar shop within the seaside down of Aberystwyth.

      Winner of the Best Dressed Shop Window competition Christmas 2014, Christmas 2017 and Christmas 2018, our window displays are constantly changing with the seasons and occasions.

      Winner of Best Start up Business in Aberystwyth by someone under the age of 25 (Menter Aberystwyth Awards 2015), Best Customer Service Award (Menter Aberystwyth Awards 2016) and Best Young Entrepreneur (Menter Aberystwyth Awards 2017).

      We have also been finalists in the BFA Florist of the Year competition 2017, The Welsh Independent Business Awards 2017 and The Welsh Wedding Awards 2017.
